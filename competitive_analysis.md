## SECTION 3: KEYWORD VOLUME REALITY CHECK

The DataForSEO keyword data shows very low Google Ads volumes (10–30/mo). **Do not be alarmed.** Here's why:

1. **Google Ads data systematically underreports local intent queries** — "near me" searches, voice searches, and queries below advertiser activity thresholds show as N/A or <10
2. **The competitors are still ranking because the searches ARE happening** — if no one searched, there would be no businesses competing for these terms
3. **The real volume is in aggregate** — 10 searches/month for "mobile oil change Sacramento" + 10 for city variants + 10 for service variants + voice/near-me searches = meaningful real-world call volume
4. **Low advertiser competition = low CPC = this is not a saturated market** — most Sacramento mobile mechanics aren't even running Google Ads

### What the Data Actually Tells Us

The top keyword "mobile mechanic Sacramento" at 30/mo in Google Ads data likely represents **200–400 actual monthly searches** when you include all query variations Google doesn't report. The business is viable — there are active competitors with reviews proving real customers are converting.
