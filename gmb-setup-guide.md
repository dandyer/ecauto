# Google Business Profile (GBP) Setup Guide
## EC Mobile Auto Services LLC — Step-by-Step

---

## BEFORE YOU START — What You'll Need
- Google account (use a dedicated business Gmail, e.g. ecmobileautoservices@gmail.com)
- Enrique's phone number (the business line — this is what customers call)
- Business address (used for verification only — will be HIDDEN publicly)
- List of cities served
- Business description (copy from Section 4 below)
- 10-15 photos ready to upload

---

## PHASE 1: CREATE THE PROFILE

### Step 1 — Go to Google Business Profile
- Go to: business.google.com
- Click **"Manage now"**
- Sign in with the business Gmail account

### Step 2 — Enter the Business Name
- Type exactly: **EC Mobile Auto Services LLC**
- If Google suggests an existing listing, check if it's a duplicate — if not, click **"Create a business with this name"**

### Step 3 — Choose Business Category
- Type "Auto Repair" in the search box
- Select: **Auto Repair Shop** (this is the closest category — "Mobile Mechanic" doesn't exist as a standalone GBP category)
- You'll add secondary categories later

### Step 4 — Do You Have a Physical Location Customers Visit?
- Select: **NO**
- This flags EC Mobile Auto as a Service Area Business (SAB) — correct for a mobile service

### Step 5 — Add Service Areas
Add ALL of the following cities (type each one and select from dropdown):
1. Sacramento, CA
2. Elk Grove, CA
3. Rancho Cordova, CA
4. Citrus Heights, CA
5. Roseville, CA
6. Folsom, CA
7. Carmichael, CA
8. Fair Oaks, CA
9. Antelope, CA
10. North Highlands, CA
11. Orangevale, CA
12. Gold River, CA
13. West Sacramento, CA
14. Rocklin, CA

> Google allows up to 20 service areas. Don't exceed a realistic 2-hour driving radius or Google may flag the profile.

### Step 6 — Enter Contact Info
- **Phone:** Enrique's business cell (this is the #1 contact method)
- **Website:** Enter the website URL once it's live. If not ready yet, you can skip and add later.

### Step 7 — Verify the Business
Google will offer several verification methods:
- **Phone call** — automated call to the business number with a code (fastest)
- **Text message** — code sent to business number (also fast)
- **Postcard** — mailed to the home address (takes 5-14 days, use only if phone fails)
- **Video** — newer option, Google may ask for a short video showing you performing work

> **Recommended:** Choose phone or text verification. Enter the code when it arrives. The profile goes live immediately after.

Go to **More → Attributes** and enable every applicable one:
- ✅ Identifies as Latino-owned (if Enrique is comfortable — boosts visibility for diversity searches)
- ✅ Appointment required
- ✅ Online appointments (once booking system is live)
- ✅ Credit cards accepted

### Step 13 — Add Photos
Upload photos in this priority order. **Label each file descriptively BEFORE uploading** (e.g., `mobile-oil-change-sacramento.jpg`):

**Upload at launch (minimum 10):**
1. Enrique working on a vehicle (action shot, best photo first)
2. Tools laid out / equipment shot
3. Oil change in progress — close-up
4. Service vehicle / truck exterior (if applicable)
5. Finished job — clean engine bay
6. Brake job in progress
7. Battery replacement photo
8. Enrique portrait / professional headshot
9. Before/after comparison (dirty vs clean oil, old vs new filter)
10. Happy customer + car (with permission)

**After launch:** Add 2-3 new photos every week. GBP rewards fresh photo activity.

**Photo tips:**
- Minimum resolution: 720px × 720px
- Take photos outdoors in natural light when possible
- Turn off timestamps/watermarks
- Take photos ON LOCATION (the GPS data in the photo metadata helps Google confirm your service area)

**Q: Can you come to my workplace parking lot?**

**Q: Do you offer same-day service?**

## PHASE 3: ONGOING MAINTENANCE (Weekly Habits)

### Every Week — Post an Update
Go to **Posts → Add Update**. Rotate between these post types:

**Service Offer Post (every other week):**
> "Need a mobile oil change in Sacramento? We come to you — at home, at work, or wherever your car is. Full synthetic starting at $XX. Book this week and save $10. Call/text Enrique: [number]"

**Photo Post (weekly):**
> Upload a job photo with a caption like: "Just wrapped up a synthetic oil change for a customer in Elk Grove — done in 35 minutes without them leaving home. Call/text to book: [number]"

**FAQ / Tip Post:**
> "Did you know Sacramento's extreme heat accelerates oil breakdown? Most manufacturers recommend oil changes every 5,000-7,500 miles for conventional oil. Not sure what yours needs? Text us!"

**Seasonal Post:**
> "Summer in Sacramento is brutal on your car's coolant system. Get a free inspection when you book any service this month. Call/text: [number]"

### Every Time You Finish a Job — Ask for a Review
Send this text message to the customer 2-3 hours after completing the job:

> "Hi [Name], it was great working on your [car] today! If you have 30 seconds, an honest Google review would mean a lot to my small business. Here's the direct link: [GBP review link]  — Enrique, EC Mobile Auto Services"

**How to get your review link:**
1. Go to your GBP dashboard
2. Click **"Get More Reviews"** (on the home screen or under Promote)
3. Copy the short link Google generates
4. Save it — you'll use this in every follow-up text

**Review response rule:** Reply to EVERY review within 24 hours.
- Positive: Thank them by name, mention the service and city, invite them back
- Negative: Acknowledge, apologize, offer to make it right — never argue

## PHASE 4: PROFILE HEALTH CHECKS (Monthly)

Run through this checklist every month:
- [ ] All business info is accurate (phone, hours, service areas)
- [ ] At least 8 new photos uploaded this month
- [ ] Posted at least 4 GBP updates this month
- [ ] Responded to all new reviews
- [ ] Q&A section has no unanswered questions
- [ ] Check GBP Insights: which search terms triggered your profile this month
- [ ] Review your photo view count — low numbers = upload more / better photos

## QUICK REFERENCE — What Google Rewards for SAB Rankings

In order of impact:
1. **Review count + recency** — get a new review every 1-2 weeks minimum
2. **Profile completeness** — fill out every field (you did this in Phase 2)
3. **Post frequency** — post every 7 days
4. **Photo freshness** — new photos weekly
5. **Q&A coverage** — answer every question fast
6. **Service area relevance** — don't overextend beyond realistic range
7. **Website link quality** — link to a fast, mobile-optimized site (build this next)
